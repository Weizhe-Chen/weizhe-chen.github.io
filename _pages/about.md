Hi! I'm Weizhe(/ˈweɪ·dʒər/) Chen, a Ph.D. student advised by [Prof. Lantao Liu](http://homes.sice.indiana.edu/lantao/) at [Intelligent Systems Engineering](https://www.engineering.indiana.edu/)(ISE), [School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering](https://www.sice.indiana.edu/), [Indiana University - Bloomington](https://www.indiana.edu/)(IUB).
I am a member of the [Vehicle Autonomy and Intelligence Lab](http://vail.sice.indiana.edu/)(VAIL) and currently focus on developing decision-making algorithms for autonomous robotic systems with potential applications in planetary exploration, environmental sampling and monitoring, as well as search and rescue. My broader research interests are in the areas of robotics, Bayesian statistics, and machine learning.

Before coming to IUB, I had a great time in Information and Computing Science, School of Mathematical Sciences, South China Normal University, advised by [Prof. Xiangru Li](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Xiangru_Li).
